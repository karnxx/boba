what is icecream?

ice cream is a cold desert. it is made by combining milk, cream, sugar, and flavorings, then freezing the mixture while churning it to incorporate air and prevent large ice crystals from forming. it is very delicious. it can have different flavours such as chocolate, vanilla, cookies and cream, strawberry, coffee, mint chip, etc. it can also cause brainfreeze. it is a semi-solid foam at low temps, such as 2deg C or about 35deg F. there are different types of ice creams such as soft-serve, sundaes, etc. ice cream can be not only made from milk but also goat milk, soy milk, almond milk. italian icecream is known as gelato, made from yogurt.
